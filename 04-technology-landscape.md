## Property Data Providers

### CoreLogic Australia (now Cotality)
- Data on **14M+ Australian properties**
- Used by **80% of top lenders** and **30,000+ professionals**
- AVM: Mean absolute error under 10%; ~90% within 15% of actual sale price
- 7,000+ data sources, 600K+ sales/year, 550K+ rental transactions/year
- RP Data is the core platform
- **Near-monopoly position in AU property data**

### PropTrack (REA Group)
- **7M+ AVM estimates per annum**
- Powers lenders covering 90%+ of Australian market
- Data from land titles offices, Valuers General, realestate.com.au
- Partnership with Ambient for noise modelling (3.7M Victorian properties)
- Acquired by REA in 2018 (formerly Hometrack)

## Credit Reporting Bureaus

| Bureau | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **Equifax** (formerly Veda) | Largest. Most widely used by banks and specialist lenders |
| **Experian** (acquired illion late 2024) | Second-largest. Combined entity |
| **illion** (formerly D&B Australia) | First to implement CCR. Now part of Experian |

**Comprehensive Credit Reporting (CCR):**
- Mandatory for major banks since 2018
- 92 credit providers participating, 22M+ accounts
- BNPL providers (Afterpay, Zip, Klarna) required from June 2025
- Application times halved at one major lender (4.9 → 1.9 days)
- One lender found they could approve 90% of previously rejected applications without increasing defaults

## The Technology Gap

Most private/non-bank lenders are still using:
- **Email** for document collection
- **Excel** for credit analysis
- **Word/PDF** for term sheets
- **Manual** cross-referencing of lender policies

The technology ecosystem is mature for mainstream residential lending (NextGen, aggregator CRMs) but **severely underdeveloped for private credit and commercial lending**.
